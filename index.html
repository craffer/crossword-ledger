    About

    A small, independent dashboard that aggregates the daily r/crossword rating polls for the New York Times Crossword. No affiliation with The Times or Reddit.

    The Rating

    Each poll option is worth 5 (Excellent), 4 (Good), 3 (Average), 2 (Poor), or 1 (Terrible). The mean excludes non-solvers.
